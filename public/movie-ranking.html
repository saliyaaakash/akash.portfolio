The Best Movies According to Akash

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Sholay (1975)

The film, directed by Ramesh Sippy, boasts of an iconic cast, unforgettable dialogues, and an everlasting villain, Gabbar Singh.

Mother India (1957)

This film by Mehboob Khan stands tall in the annals of Indian cinema, embodying the true essence of motherhood.

Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge (1995)

Directed by Aditya Chopra, this film set new standards for romance and celebrated the victory of love over all obstacles.
